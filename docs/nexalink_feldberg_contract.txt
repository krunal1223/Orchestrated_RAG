ARTICLE 4 — SUPPORT AND SERVICE LEVELS
=======================================================

4.1 Platinum Support Includes:
- 24/7/365 technical support via dedicated phone hotline and email
- Maximum response times: P1 (Critical) = 30 minutes; P2 (High) = 2 hours; P3 (Medium) = 8 hours; P4 (Low) = 48 hours
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM): Ms. Divya Rao, reachable at d.rao@nexalink.com
- Quarterly business reviews
- Early access to beta releases (minimum 60 days before general availability)

4.2 Uptime SLA: Licensor guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for cloud-hosted components (excluding Licensee's AWS infrastructure and scheduled maintenance). Scheduled maintenance windows: Sundays 02:00–06:00 CET with 72 hours advance notice.

4.3 SLA Credits: If uptime falls below 99.9%, Licensee receives service credits:
- 99.0%–99.8%: 10% credit on monthly fee
- 95.0%–98.9%: 25% credit on monthly fee
- Below 95.0%: 50% credit on monthly fee
Credits are the sole remedy for SLA breaches; Licensee waives any other claims for downtime.

4.4 Exclusions from SLA: Downtime caused by Licensee's infrastructure failures, Force Majeure events, or Licensee's failure to apply critical patches within 30 days of release.

=======================================================

5.2 Data Location: All Licensee data is stored exclusively in AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt). No data transfer outside the European Economic Area without written consent.

5.3 Data Retention: Upon termination, Licensor retains Licensee data for 90 days in read-only archive format, after which it is permanently deleted. 5.4 Security: Licensor maintains ISO 27001 certification. Annual penetration tests conducted by independent third party. Licensor must notify Licensee of any security breach affecting Licensee data within 48 hours of discovery.

ARTICLE 8 — TERM AND TERMINATION
=======================================================

8.1 Initial Term: 3 years from August 1, 2024 to July 31, 2027.

8.2 Renewal: Agreement automatically renews for successive 1-year terms unless either party gives 90 days written notice of non-renewal before the end of any term.

8.3 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:
(a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice

(c) Ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course

8.4 Effect of Termination:
(a) All licenses granted immediately cease
(b) Licensee must destroy all copies of the Software within 15 days and certify destruction in writing
(c) Outstanding payment obligations survive termination
(d) Data export rights per Article 5.3 survive for 90 days

8.5 Termination for Convenience: Licensee may terminate for convenience with 6 months written notice, but is not entitled to a refund of prepaid fees.
